You found the identity of the hacker, Mickel Fritz, and you also found where the data thrown was placed!

But... You don't have access to it and you don't have any personnal information which allows the hacker to be arrested.

These informations are in the same storage part of Fritz's computer, which is protected with a key!

So, in order to get access to these informations, your only option, as a detective, is to find the key with the solution of a riddle!
